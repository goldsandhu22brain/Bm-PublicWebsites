What is certification?

Term certification is defined as, “Certifications are special qualifications a person can obtain to demonstrate their reliability and ability to carry out a profession. Your certification is often presented as a document that certifies that, as a professional, you have received the necessary training, education, and preparation to fulfill the requirements of your position.” Certifications hold great relevance in hiring and recruiting staff because Team members must communicate and have the same degree of skill. Companies started valuing certificates to address this problem. Certifications are used to demonstrate candidates' abilities and ensure that their education is standardized.

Bottom-line is a certification is a tool that bridges the gap that recruiters face while hiring which is the capability of an individual to handle real-time job responsibilities.

Reputed certification bodies like Brainmeasures provide a battery of assessments from Psychometric tests, IT and non-technical skills, coding challenges, hackathons, and online interviews all in one place and a single easy-to-use dashboard, AI and ML-based reporting system, API integration for seamless testing, automatic and human proctoring services that make the system the entire system reliable and authentic and helping recruiters make right hiring decisions and building a great team of certified professionals for their organizations.
