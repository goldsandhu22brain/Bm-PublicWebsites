Pre-Employment Solutions

Brainstormed by the Team Brainmeasures, Brainmeasures is a comprehensive online training certification and skill-assessment mechanism that is diligently assisting worldwide Employers to seamlessly connect, assess, quantify, and appoint the Best Caliber for their diverse Workforce Needs.

Trusted Global Enterprises as well as aspiring individuals and job-seekers, Brainmeasures offers an extensive portfolio of online skill-testing assessments and coding challenges, along with an exclusive range of online training, skill-practicing, and certification programs.

Objective

Mphasis was looking to expand its ‘Application Management Team’ wanted to add experienced JAVA Coders who are capable of developing advanced applications and embedded solutions utilizing technologies, including JAVA/J2EE, Spring MVC, Spring Transaction, and Hibernate, as specified by Mphasis’s business partners. Mphasis also wanted to build its goodwill as a potential employer for the participants.

Challenge

Mphasis' hiring team approached the Brainmeasures experts to:

  • Assess and appoint experienced JAVA Developers who can develop and support J2EE Applications and services
  • Integrate its own skill assessments, including Coding Problems and aptitude tests
  • Promote its brand through HackerHac’s advanced online skill testing platform.

Solution

Experts @ Brainmeasures helped Mphasis by:
  • Devising a user-friendly skill-test creation interface for the Mphasis’s recruiters to upload and integrate their own skill assessments.
  • Enabling recruiters to include relevant company information as well as the company’s LOGO with the skill assessment, to promote Mphasis
The smart online skill testing module by Brainmeasures enabled Mphasis’ hiring team to:
  • Integrate Mphasis’ own skill tests that, include Multiple Choice Questions and advanced Coding Problems
  • Seamlessly assess, shortlist, and appoint best talent with desired expertise
  • Uplifting the Company’s brand goodwill
