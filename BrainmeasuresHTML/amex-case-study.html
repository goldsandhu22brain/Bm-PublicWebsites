Brainmeasures online skill assessments are helping AmEx

Advanced
Pre-Employment Solutions

Brainstormed by the Team Brainmeasures, Brainmeasures is a comprehensive online training certification and skill-assessment mechanism that is diligently assisting worldwide Employers to seamlessly connect, assess, quantify, and appoint the Best Caliber for their diverse Workforce Needs.

Trusted Global Enterprises as well as aspiring individuals and job-seekers, Brainmeasures offers an extensive portfolio of online skill-testing assessments and coding challenges, along with an exclusive range of online training, skill-practicing, and certification programs.

Objective

Talent Hunt Managers at AmEx were aspired to transform the AmEx’s existing employee hiring framework leveraging Brainmeasures’ advanced online skill-testing and recruitment mechanism as well as intelligent online proctoring system.

Challenge

AmEx's hiring team approached the Brainmeasures experts to:

  • Save time for assessing and appointing the best talent from a large pool of Information Technology (IT) specialists for various AmEx’s locations
  • Ensure the authenticity of the candidates participated for the skill assessment

Solution

Experts @ Brainmeasures helped AmEx by:
  • Devising an intelligent online skill-assessment and hiring platform
  • Integrating advanced coding problems and smart Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) to evaluate the participants AI related programming expertise
  • Incorporating an indigenously developed features-loaded automated proctoring system
The smart online skill testing module by Brainmeasures enabled AmEx’s hiring team to:
  • Replace the AmEx’s complicated and time consuming recruitment platform with Brainmeasures’ new fast online skill-assessment and talent acquisition mechanism
  • Identify and hire the best IT talent for AmEx’s offices across India
  • Ensuring that the skill-assessment was authentic and unbiased

Outcome

  1. Hiring Process TAT (Turn-Around-Time): Reduced by nearly

    57%

  2. Pre-Recruitment Expenses: Reduced by about

    54%

  3. Operational Cost for Hiring Process: Reduced by nearly

    59%

  4. Staffing / Recruiting Teams' Efforts: Reduced by about

    56%

  5. Hiring Rate: Improved from about 18% to nearly

    53%

  • Among the finalists, more than 300 participants were shortlisted for the final interview rounds that AmEx conducted at its various offices in India.
