Brainmeasures online skill assessments are helping Capgemini

Advanced
Pre-Employment Solutions

Brainstormed by the Team Brainmeasures, Brainmeasures is a comprehensive online training certification and skill-assessment mechanism that is diligently assisting worldwide Employers to seamlessly connect, assess, quantify, and appoint the Best Caliber for their diverse Workforce Needs.

Trusted Global Enterprises as well as aspiring individuals and job-seekers, Brainmeasures offers an extensive portfolio of online skill-testing assessments and coding challenges, along with an exclusive range of online training, skill-practicing, and certification programs.

Objective

Capgemini hires numerous Information Technology (IT) professionals through a mandatory IT skills assessment program, enabling it to assess and recruit quality talent, that include IT consultants and engineers. For such technical and seasoned job positions, Capgemini intended to evaluate advanced IT skills, including Artificial Intelligence (AI), Block-Chain, Machine Learning, and others, leveraging the online pre-employment testing solutions.

Challenge

Capgemini's hiring team approached the Brainmeasures experts to:

  • Transition its existing offline hiring process, that proved to be incompetent, into an automated online recruitment mechanism
  • Enable internal recruiters to save their time and efforts in recruiting the desired talent
  • Ensure best Returns on Investment (ROI) on employee hiring budgets that the Company allocates every year for various recruitment initiatives
  • Eliminate the added burden of manual assessments for talent acquisition

Solution

Post analyzing Capgemini’s existing employee recruitment process, Brainmeasures experts devised and delivered an advanced cloud-enabled skill-testing and talent hiring mechanism that include various smart features:
  • Browser based Coding Simulator enabled hiring managers to seamlessly assess the candidates’ technical skills
  • Mammoth Pool of Coding Problems enabled the recruiters to assess candidates on more than 100 programming skills;
  • Automated Online Proctoring with Various Future Ready Options, like face detection, face recognition, biometrics patterns, and locked test window enabled Capgemini to ensure fairness of the skill assessment without spending huge amounts on human proctoring;
  • Real Time Test Reporting with Advanced Analytics enabled recruiting managers to quickly and effectively assess and hire the best talent;
Through Brainmeasures scalable and consistent Online Skill Assessments Capgemini could efficiently conduct Walk-ins and Recruitment drives 24x7x365.

Outcome

  1. Hiring Process TAT (Turn-Around-Time): Reduced by nearly

    64%

  2. Pre-Recruitment Expenses: Reduced by about

    67%

  3. Operational Cost for Hiring Process: Reduced by nearly

    69%

  4. Staffing / Recruiting Teams' Efforts: Reduced by about

    71%

  5. Hiring Rate: Improved from about 12% to nearly

    66%
